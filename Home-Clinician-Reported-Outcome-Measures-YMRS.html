YMRS

The YMRS is a psychiatric instrument that is used to assess the severity of manic symptoms in individuals, typically in clinical or research settings. It consists of 11 items evaluating mood, speech, motor activity, sleep, and other behaviors associated with mania. Items are either scored on a scale from 0 to 4 or 0 to 8, depending on the symptom's weight. Higher scores indicate greater severity.
