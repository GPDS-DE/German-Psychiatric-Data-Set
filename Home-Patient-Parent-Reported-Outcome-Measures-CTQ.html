CTQ

The CTQ is a widely used tool to assess a history of childhood trauma. It consists of 28 items that evaluate experiences of physical, emotional, and sexual abuse, as well as physical and emotional neglect. Each item is rated on a 5-point Likert scale, from "never true" (1) to "very often true" (5), with higher scores indicating more frequent or severe trauma experiences. The total score is calculated by summing the item scores, and specific subscale scores are also derived for different types of trauma.
