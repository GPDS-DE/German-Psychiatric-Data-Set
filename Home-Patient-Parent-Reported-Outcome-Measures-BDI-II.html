BDI-II

The BDI-II is used to record and assess depressive symptoms. It consists of 21 questions, each scored on a scale from 0 to 3. The response options vary depending on the question but typically range from "not at all" or "minimal symptoms" (0 points) to "severe symptoms" (3 points). The total score is calculated by summing the points from all 21 questions, resulting in a range from 0 to 63. The evaluation is based on the total score, which corresponds to different levels of depression severity.

The German version of the BDI-II is copyrighted by Pearson. We still provided an instance based on a questionnaire we found online. The BDI-II version is accessible as a PDF.
